## 1. How to Contribute

1. [Fork](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/quickstart/fork-a-repo) the repository [ossf/security-insights-spec](https://github.com/ossf/security-insights-spec) to your own GitHub account.
2. Make changes or improvements to the specification document in your forked repository.
3. Create a [Pull Request](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/quickstart/opening-a-pull-request) with a clear title and description of your changes.

## 2. Issue Reporting

If you find issues or inconsistencies in the specification, please [open an issue](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/quickstart/opening-an-issue) with a detailed description.

## 3. Review Process

Our team will review your contributions and provide feedback. Once approved, we'll merge your changes.

Reach out to us on [Slack](https://openssf.slack.com/messages/security_insights) or join a [community meeting](https://calendar.google.com/calendar?cid=czYzdm9lZmhwNWk5cGZsdGI1cTY3bmdwZXNAZ3JvdXAuY2FsZW5kYXIuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbQ) for the Metrics and Metadata Working Group.

## 4. Code of Conduct

Please adhere to our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/ossf/.github/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) when participating in this project.

## 5. Licensing

By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the [OpenSSF SECURITY-INSIGHTS specification's license](https://github.com/ossf/security-insights-spec/blob/main/LICENSE.md).
